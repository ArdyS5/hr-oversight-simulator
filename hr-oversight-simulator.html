GOVERNANCE NOTE — This simulator demonstrates two applied concepts: (1) EU AI Act Article 14 human oversight requirements, by routing higher-impact or lower-confidence agentic HR decisions through a mandatory human checkpoint rather than full automation; and (2) NIST AI RMF "Govern" function, by making the decision policy (risk tiers, thresholds) configurable and auditable rather than fixed inside the model. The pay-compression logic specifically models proactive fairness monitoring — flagging under-market pay only where it intersects with sustained high performance, and explicitly suppressing action on average performers below midpoint, so the audit trail shows the system reasoning about who the policy applies to, not applying it uniformly.
